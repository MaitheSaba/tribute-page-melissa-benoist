"Life doesn't have to be as hard as we make it for ourselves a lot of the time."

Biography

Melissa Marie Benoist, born on October 4, 1988, is an American actress, singer, producer and writer. She was born in Houston, Texas. She started dance classes at the age of three, focusing on jazz, ballet, and tap. When she was four years old, her aunt put her in a church play she was directing; after that, she began doing community children's theater in her hometown.
